# AI scenario helper

**AI scenario helper** is an AI assistant built into the Latenode Visual Builder. Describe your task in plain language and the assistant builds the scenario: it places nodes on the canvas, connects them, and fills in settings where possible.

## What it can do

**Build scenarios.** Describe the task - the assistant proposes a structure and places nodes on the canvas. Refine, edit, add conditions right in the chat.

**Answer questions.** Ask about any node, have it explain a scenario, or suggest improvements. The assistant answers in the context of what is open on the canvas.

**Debug.** Something went wrong - describe the problem. The assistant finds where it broke and suggests how to fix it.

## How to work with it

1. **Describe your goal** — what triggers the scenario, what data is involved, and what the result should be.
2. **Monitor the build** — the assistant shows progress as it plans, selects nodes, connects them, and configures settings.
3. **Review and refine** — verify credentials, required inputs, and any assumptions; ask the assistant to adjust as needed.

## Billing

Work done by the **AI scenario helper** is billed using **PnP tokens**.
